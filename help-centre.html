For Practitioners

What is HealthDataLab and how does it work?
HealthDataLab is a health data platform built for naturopaths, herbalists, nutritionists and holistic practitioners. You invite your client to complete a detailed health questionnaire before their appointment. We generate a visual longevity report highlighting key areas to explore — so you can research their specific imbalances, prepare with confidence, and deepen the consultation from minute one.
How do I invite a client to complete a health form?
From your practitioner dashboard, you can send a client invite via email. They'll receive a magic link that takes them straight to the health assessment form — no account setup needed on their end. Once they complete the form, you'll see their data appear in your dashboard automatically.
What's included in the longevity report?
Each longevity report includes a biological age estimation, a health radar chart covering key lifestyle domains (nutrition, exercise, sleep, stress, social connection, and more), a health trajectory projection, and personalised insights based on the client's form responses. It's designed to give you a clear snapshot of where your client stands before the consultation.
How do credits work?
Each longevity report costs one credit. Your practitioner plan includes a generous pool of credits that are shared across all your clients. When a client completes their form and a report is generated, one credit is deducted from your pool. You can track your usage from your dashboard.
Can my client update their form after submitting?
Yes. Clients can log back in and update their health form at any time. Each new submission generates a fresh report, allowing you to track changes over time and monitor their progress across consultations.

For Individuals

How do I get my personal longevity report?
Visit our Personal Reports page and choose a plan. After checkout, you'll receive a welcome email with a magic link. Click it to access your health assessment form. Once you complete the form, your personalised longevity report will be generated and emailed to you as a PDF.
How long does it take to receive my report?
After you complete the health assessment form, your report is typically generated and emailed to you within a few minutes. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for the PDF. If you don't receive it within an hour, please get in touch.
Who can see my health data?
Your data is private by default. If you chose to be connected with a practitioner during checkout, only that practitioner can view your health data through their dashboard. You can read more about how we handle your data in our Privacy Policy.
What if I want to work with a practitioner afterwards?
You can connect with a practitioner at any time. When filling out your health form, you'll have the option to enter a practitioner's email address. They'll be able to view your data and report through their dashboard, making your consultations more focused and productive.

Account & Billing

How do I log in to my account?
You'll receive a magic link in your welcome email — click it to log in instantly, no password needed. If you've lost the email, check your spam folder. You can also log in directly at healthdatalab.net using the email address you registered with.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on Course and Signature tiers — no questions asked. For the Minimum tier, you can cancel anytime; access continues until the end of your billing period. The Launchpad tier is free with no financial obligation. Full details are in our Terms of Service.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription at any time by contacting us. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. We don't believe in making it hard to leave — if you'd like to cancel, just send us a message and we'll take care of it.
I didn't receive my welcome email — what should I do?
First, check your spam or junk folder — the email comes from office@healthdatalab.com. If it's not there, please get in touch with the email address you used during checkout and we'll resend your magic link right away.
