The Longevity Trajectory Protocol

Four steps that layer onto your existing practice.

MAP

Establish the baseline.

Your client completes a comprehensive longevity assessment. Far more than a blood test.

READ

Interpret with your clinical lens.

Connect the data to the whole person. This is where your training becomes indispensable.

GUIDE

Design the intervention.

A standalone consultation, an add-on, or a structured programme. You choose.

TRACK

Make progress visible.

Reassess at intervals. Show clients their trajectory improving. Motivation becomes intrinsic.
