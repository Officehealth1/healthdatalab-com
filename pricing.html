Why refunds instead of discounts?

Because discounts attract people looking for a deal. Refunds attract people willing to commit first and be rewarded for showing up.

The beta and founding cohorts need practitioners who will engage deeply — do the work, give honest feedback, and help us build the best version of this course. That’s worth more than a discount.

The engagement bar isn’t high. Complete the modules, participate in sessions, use the Protocol with at least one client, and share what worked and what didn’t. That’s it.

If you do that, you get the refund. If you ghost, you paid full price for a course that’s worth full price. No one loses.

What you’re actually getting

  • More personal involvement — smaller group, direct access, faster feedback loops
  • More depth — you’re helping shape the curriculum, not just consuming it
  • Signature-tier elements — beta participants get features normally reserved for the Signature plan
