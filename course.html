The Certificate

The certificate is awarded at the end of the full 12 weeks — not automatically, but after you’ve demonstrated a working understanding of the Protocol and have applied (or genuinely attempted to apply) the recommendations with real clients.

This isn’t a participation trophy. It’s a professional credential that signals you can actually do this work. Passing criteria: consistent engagement throughout the course, evidence of using the Protocol in practice, and a level of understanding that shows you’re ready to deliver longevity consultations independently.

The certificate matters because it protects the quality of what it represents — for you and for every practitioner who holds one.
