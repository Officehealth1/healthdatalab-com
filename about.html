The Story

Matthew has spent over 25 years working as a naturopath and iridologist. Not building courses about it. Not talking about it on social media. Actually doing it — sitting across from real people, reading their eyes, asking the right questions, and figuring out what their body was trying to say. That kind of time with clients teaches you something no textbook can: what practitioners actually need to do their best work.

With a background in engineering, Matthew brings a precision to health that most practitioners don’t have access to. That analytical lens — applied to 25 years of clinical observation — is what eventually produced the Longevity Trajectory Protocol.

That understanding led to IRISLAB.com and Iris Mapper — tools built from the inside out, by someone who uses them. Today, over 2,000 practitioners worldwide rely on IRISLAB in their daily practice. Building that platform over more than a decade taught Matthew something else: holistic practitioners are brilliant at seeing the whole person, but they have almost no way to prove it with data. Conventional medicine has labs, imaging, biomarkers. Holistic practice has intuition and experience — powerful, but invisible to anyone outside the room.

HealthDataLab exists to close that gap. The Longevity Trajectory Protocol came from a pattern Matthew saw again and again: practitioners who could clearly see what was going on with a client but had no structured way to track it, measure it, or show progress over time. The platform turns that clinical insight into something visible — a data layer that sits alongside the practitioner's own expertise, not instead of it.

This isn't a business play. It's a 25-year obsession that finally has the right tools to match. The goal has always been the same: give holistic practitioners the same data credibility that conventional medicine takes for granted. Not by turning them into something they're not, but by proving what they already do — with numbers that speak for themselves.

Why holistic practitioners — and why now

Health isn’t just biochemistry. Beliefs, emotions, identity — they have measurable physiological impact. Placebo and nocebo research makes this clear.

Clinical longevity gives people numbers. Holistic practitioners give them meaning. That combination — precision and depth — creates change that lasts.

HealthDataLab was built specifically for holistic practitioners, not doctors or fitness coaches. Because practitioners who already understand the whole person are the ones best positioned to lead in longevity.

The Protocol provides the data. You provide the wisdom to connect it to the person’s life. That’s the deep roots concept — lasting health change comes from identity, not just knowledge.
